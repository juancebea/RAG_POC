# V13RNES Fest

V13RNES Fest is an independent heavy music event organized in Córdoba, Argentina.

The festival combines underground music, horror-inspired aesthetics, community promotion, and local scene collaboration.

## V13RNES Fest Vol. 3

V13RNES Fest Vol. 3 took place on December 6, 2024 at Sala Formosa.

The lineup included Álamo, Sveta, Ternarium, and Sha-Fu.

The edition had a doom metal and slasher-inspired theme.

## V13RNES Fest Vol. 5

The V13RNES Fest Vol. 5 campaign was planned around a 30-day content strategy.

The content strategy included lineup reveals, band interviews, ticket announcements, merch content, playlist interactions, and short social media videos.

The campaign considered using the Sha-Fu Instagram account because it had a larger audience than the V13RNES Fest account.

The host persona Oni was considered for interviews and reels.
