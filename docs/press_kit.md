# Sha-Fu Press Kit

Sha-Fu is available for live shows, festivals, support slots, and international band support opportunities.

The band is based in Córdoba, Argentina.

Sha-Fu can communicate in Spanish and English.

Sha-Fu's live setup is designed to be professional, direct, and efficient for venue production teams.

## Booking angle

Sha-Fu is suitable for progressive metal, doom metal, post-metal, instrumental rock, and experimental heavy music events.

The band can work as support for international bands that fit heavy, progressive, atmospheric, or alternative lineups.

## Production notes

Sha-Fu can provide a technical rider and stage plot.

The band uses direct guitar and bass routing, in-ear monitoring, backing tracks, click, cues, and a compact digital mixer-based setup.
